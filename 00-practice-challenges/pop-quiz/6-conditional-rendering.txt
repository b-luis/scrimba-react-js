1. What is "conditional rendering"?

    We render something based on whether it is truthy or falsy.

    Scrimba Answer: When we want to only sometimes display something on the page based on a condition of some sort

2. When would you use &&?

    If you want to display something that satisfies a TRUTHY condition.

    Scrimba Answer: When you want to either display something or NOT display it.

3. When would you use a ternary?

    If you want to display either one of the condition.

    Scrimba Answer: When you need to decide which thing among 2 options to display

4. What if you need to decide between > 2 options on
   what to display?

   Just use the good old way if..else.
